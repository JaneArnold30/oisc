Memberships

We have three types of memberships:

  1. Full OISC Member - these are full voting members of the Ocean Islanders Shag Club.

    Member Form

  2. Affiliated Associate Member - these are non-voting members from other shag clubs who attend our dance for a reduced rate.

    Affiliated Associate Member Form

  3. Unaffiliated Associate Member -these are non-voting members who do not belong to any other shag clubs.
